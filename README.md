## ✦ Research

**Finding MEMO: The Hidden Influence of Memorization in LLMs' Performance**
*University of Khartoum · Supervised by Prof Sharief F. Babiker · Sep 2024 – Jun 2025*

> **Accepted — WiML @ NeurIPS, December 2025**

Constructed **ALSA-5K** — a benchmark of 5,000 math problems to disentangle genuine reasoning from memorization in LLMs. Fine-tuned Qwen2.5-1.5B across four controlled scenarios; diverse training outperformed memorization-only baselines by **+4.8%** and revealed a clear transfer learning asymmetry: models trained on diverse data adapt significantly better to repetitive evaluation setups.

*Equitech Scholars Program · Supervised by Dr Bhasi Nair · Aug 2023*

Integrated 3 heterogeneous real-world datasets. Applied logarithmic transformations, multiplicative modelling, and confounding-variable controls to uncover nonlinear cross-country patterns.
