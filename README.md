## ✦ Research

> **Finding MEMO: The Hidden Influence of Memorization in Large Language Models' Performance**
> *University of Khartoum · Sep 2024 – Jun 2025*
>
> `Accepted — WiML @ NeurIPS, December 2025`

Constructed **ALSA-5K** — a benchmark of 5,000 math problems to disentangle genuine reasoning from memorization in LLMs. Fine-tuned Qwen2.5-1.5B across four controlled scenarios; diverse training outperformed memorization-only baselines by **+4.8%** and revealed a clear transfer learning asymmetry.

---

> **Exploratory Analysis: Drug Use Disorder × Social Media Usage**
> *Equitech Scholars Program · Aug 2023*

Integrated 3 heterogeneous real-world datasets. Applied logarithmic transformations, multiplicative modelling, and confounding-variable controls to uncover nonlinear cross-country patterns.
